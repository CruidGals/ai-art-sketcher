AI Art Helper Sketcher

Premise:

In the growing age of technology, recognizing the importance of balancing AI and human created content is crucial. My issue brief was written to inform policymakers about the urgent need to regulate the influx of AI content on the internet. This website is meant to supplement the brief; it is a hands-on activity that combines the creativity of the human mind with the productivity of an AI machine. While simple and imperfect, it represents the larger picture for what an ideal world with AI looks like.

Start drawing on the white canvas below with you mouse!

Tip: Drawing fewer strokes makes the AI draw more.

To let AI take over for a few strokes, hit the Start Sketching button.
To change what the AI should focus on drawing, change the model in the dropdown box.
When you are done with the sketch, hit the Finish with AI button to turn your sketch into an AI masterpiece!
